Welcome to my page

I'm a graduate student at the University of Chicago in the Digital Studies program. I studied at Bowdoin College in the great state of Maine for undergrad, where I majored in Political Theory and English and minored in Ancient Greek. My research interests include British Romantic poetry, literary theory, Nietzsche's aesthetics and politics, Schiller's aesthetics and politics, and linguistics and the Sublime.
